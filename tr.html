Frequently Asked Questions

What is a tournament rating (TR)?

It's a results based rating that shows how well you've performed in Kentucky tournaments. It functions much like ELO. Everyone starts with a rating of 1500.0. After each tournament, all the matches are recorded and a new TR is determined for each participant. Each match, the difference in TR is calculated and the winner is rewarded TR while the loser loses TR. TR is zero sum, so the amount gained/lost per match is the same for both people. If the favorite of a match wins, TR changes less. And if the underdog wins, the TR will change more.

How often is TR updated?

TR is updated after every tournament. But the leaderboard will only be updated at the beginning of each month. To appear on the leaderboard, a player must have played in 5 tournaments in that calendar year.

Are all tournaments used for TR?

All double elimination tournaments are used for TR, but not all tournaments are equal. Tournaments with tougher competition will have higher potential to gain TR.

Do out of state competitors get a TR?

Out of state competitors (or invaders) do not get a permanent rating or get placement on the leaderboards. Instead, they are given a rating per tournament based on the highest rated player who competed and the invader's placement in that tournament. If you notice someone is labeled as an invader but shouldn't be or vice versa, please let me know either by Twitter or E-Mail.

Are there resets or seasons?

The current plan is to start a new leaderboard at the beginning of every year. That being said, it won't be a complete reset. It will be more of a rating squishing. TRs will be halved by their distance from 1500.0. So a player with a TR of 1700.0 would be pushed back to 1600.0. And a player with a TR of 1400.0 would be pushed to 1450.0. Additionally, players won't appear on the new year's leaderboard unless they play in a eligible tournament in the new year. Any player who misses a full year of competition will have their rating reset.

Why isn't there a TR Leaderboard for [insert game]?

If it's a popular game, I'm probably working on it. I want to have a TR for every game that is played in Kentucky. I'm currently working backwards with the newer games first.
